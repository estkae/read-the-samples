The UT Dallas Student Government Association (SGA) is an organization made up of students that advocates for the student body, organizes various campus events, and leads student initiatives. The SGA is comprised of various committees, of which committees you can join by contacting the respective Chair of the committee.\n\nWe are always looking for new members, especially for our campus committees.
