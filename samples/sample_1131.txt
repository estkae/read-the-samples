International School Manila\n\nThe International School Manila (ISM) is an international school on Red Oak Place, in Bonifacio Global City, Taguig City, Metro Manila, Philippines. It offers an American-based curriculum with a British orientation and is recognized by the United States Department of State, British Council, and Council of International Schools.\n\nIn 1979, the school opened as the Manila International School and was accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC) and British Schools Overseas (BSO). ISM became an IB World School in 1989. In 1998, the school was renamed International School Manila to reflect the school's expanded reach outside the Philippines and its increasing number of non-Filipino nationals on its teaching staff.
