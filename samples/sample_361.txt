JUMBLE CONFIDENCE\n\nDefinitions\n\nConfidence: A term describing the likelihood that a decision-making system will reach a good decision. It refers to the quality of a decision, not to the quality of the process that leads to that decision.\n\nJumble: A pattern of naturalistic decision making. It does not include extraneous information or create false information.\n\nExample\n\nA fighter pilot is confident that she will destroy the enemy aircraft even though she has only her own memory of previous aerial encounters to rely on.
