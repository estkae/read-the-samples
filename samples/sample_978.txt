LOWELL — Police have arrested a Lowell woman on charges she left her 10-year-old daughter unattended in a vehicle at an Old Country Buffet restaurant, then left the girl in the restaurant and drove away.\n\nNahilie Estela, 36, of Lowell, was arrested on two counts of reckless endangerment of a child. She is scheduled to be arraigned in Lowell District Court on Oct. 2.\n\nA police report said a restaurant employee, who was on her break, noticed a small child sitting in the car and called police. The report said Estela went into the restaurant and left the girl in the vehicle.\n\nPolice said Estela told them she thought her daughter was with her friend.
