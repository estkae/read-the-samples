A neighborhood staple since 2003, Shawarma Grill offers quality Mediterranean cuisine, including an extensive menu of vegetarian and vegan options. Customers rave about the huge portions, reasonable prices, and friendly staff. If you're looking for healthy options, Shawarma Grill has salads, burgers, and baked potatoes on the menu. The vegetarian gyro is another hit, and the shawarma is sure to please any meat lover.
