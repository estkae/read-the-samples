The Don's Analyst\n\nThe Don's Analyst (, , ) is a 1968 Italian-French comedy film directed by Sergio Corbucci.\n\nDon Salvatore's sister wants to marry Don Vincenzo's son, which makes the two crime families form a union. But Don Salvatore's son is in love with the Don's brother-in-law's daughter, which makes the two families' alliance a difficult one.\n\n\n
