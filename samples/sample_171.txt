"Casa Italia (band)\n\nCasa Italia was a short-lived Italian band.\n\nCasa Italia was born in 1988 in Bologna as a project for the creation of music without any commercial purpose. The initial core of the band is formed by Daniele Chiodi, Franco Stalteri and Valerio Varesi, and the experience is enriched by other musicians who will be only present for live performances.\n\nIn the years from 1988 to 1995, Casa Italia published three full-length albums, "Infinito in te" (1991), "Sono come sono" (1992), "Gli dei della gioia" (1995), the last two of which were released by the MTM Music label.\n\n\n
