Faythe Levine is the co-founder of Roadside America, a company dedicated to publishing and producing books on the unique, the quirky, and the offbeat in the United States. A native of rural Maine, Levine received a BA in art history from the University of Maine in 2001, followed by a MA in history from Columbia University in 2002. Her research interests focus on environmental history and the politics of tourism, particularly in the United States and Europe.
