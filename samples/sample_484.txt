A 21-year-old man died early Tuesday morning after being struck by a car while riding his bike in Logan Square, police said.\n\nAt 1:26 a.m., authorities responded to the intersection of Fullerton and Armitage avenues where the bicyclist was hit by a car, according to Chicago Police.
