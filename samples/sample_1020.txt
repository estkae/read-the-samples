Webb Memorial Chapel\n\nWebb Memorial Chapel is a historic church building in Atwood, Tennessee, that is listed on the National Register of Historic Places.\n\nThe chapel was built in 1893 by the Union Sunday School of Baptist Church. It was added to the National Register in 1995.\n\nThe chapel was moved from the Atwood city center to the southwest side of town in 1983 to its current location at 685 Station Rd.\n
