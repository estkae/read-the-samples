"New Delhi: A Delhi court has sent former Union minister Santosh Bagrodia to judicial custody till January 18 after the Enforcement Directorate (ED) produced him in a money laundering case.\n\nSpecial Judge Naresh Kumar Malhotra ordered that Bagrodia, a former Union steel minister, be sent to judicial custody.\n\nBagrodia was arrested in the morning hours by the ED officials.\n\nThe ED had lodged a money laundering case against him in 2013 for his alleged involvement in money laundering in a power project in Jharkhand.\n\nThe agency had also arrested Rajya Sabha MP Anil Sharma, also an accused in the case, earlier this week.\n\nBagrodia had resigned from the Union Cabinet in the middle of 2013.
