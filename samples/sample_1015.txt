José Alves\n\nJosé Alves (born 23 March 1949) is a retired Portuguese footballer who played as a left back.\n\nDuring a career which spanned 18 seasons, he played for Estoril, Benfica, União de Tomar, Belenenses and Académico Porto.\n\nHe earned 21 caps for Portugal, making his debut against West Germany in 1971. He represented his country in 3 FIFA World Cup qualifying matches.\n\n
