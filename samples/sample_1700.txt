Andy Cohen\n\nIn 2011, Cohen made his debut as the host of the Bravo reality television series "Watch What Happens Live", a live, late-night show in which he talks about pop culture, celebrity gossip and his own personal life, which he had previously done as host of "The Real Housewives After Show". It also has been reported that Cohen has made a deal with Bravo to do three to four episodes of his own talk show a year.
