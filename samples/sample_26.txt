University: The primary mission of the University is to provide education. For that purpose, the University has established different faculties and departments, of which the oldest and most important is the faculty of theology. The latter is a privileged entity, by virtue of its academic reputation, both in the local and the global sphere. The mission of the University is to educate the professors and the students in the science of theology, which means, it also needs to equip them with the specific professional skills, which make them able to exercise the profession of the ministry.
