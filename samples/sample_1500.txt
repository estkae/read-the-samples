"Meghasandesham\n\nMeghasandesham is a 1990 Indian Malayalam film, directed by Hariharan and produced by Madhupal. The film stars Jagadish, Vineeth, Captain Raju and Ranjini in lead roles. The film had musical score by Vidyadharan.\n\n\nThe music was composed by Vidyadharan and lyrics was written by Kaithapram. \n
