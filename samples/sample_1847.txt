"Gregory W. Funk (born in 1945) is an American historian of religion, and Joseph B. Wadsworth Professor of Religion and Theology Emeritus at the University of Notre Dame. Funk is a leading scholar on the historical Jesus, and has authored many books including, The Gospel of Jesus: According to the Jesus Seminar (1997), The Acts of Jesus: What Did Jesus Really Do? A Historical Examination of the Bible's Earliest Acts (2006), and Honest to Jesus: Jesus for a New Millennium (1998). He is a co-founder of the Jesus Seminar and is on the editorial board of the academic journal, Dead Sea Discoveries. Funk is a prolific public speaker, frequently traveling to give lectures on his work.\n\n
