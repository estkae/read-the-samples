China National Highway 308\n\nChina National Highway 308 (G308) runs from Dandong, Liaoning to Yanji, Yanbian, Jilin. It is 2,070 kilometres in length and runs north from Dandong to the border with North Korea, crossing the Yellow Sea via the Dandong Bridge.
