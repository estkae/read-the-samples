About “[Go]”\n\nDoo Wop–esque song by The Weeknd.\n\nIts sound is very similar to the 50s and 60s; therefore it is likely that this song would have been the introduction to The Weeknd’s debut mixtape, “House of Balloons.”\n\nThe song was written and produced by The Weeknd, with co-production by Doc McKinney and Illangelo.\n\nThe Weeknd has stated in several interviews that the song was written about one of his ex-girlfriends.
