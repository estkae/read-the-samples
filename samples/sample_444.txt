Alejandro Leguizamón\n\nAlejandro Leguizamón (born October 30, 1961 in Santa Fe) is an Argentine journalist. He is a columnist of "Noticias" and "Página/12". Leguizamón also is a member of "The Clinic", a leading group of Argentine intellectuals, and a writer of a weekly column in "Página/12".\n\nAlejandro Leguizamón is the son of Eduardo Leguizamón, founder of the "Círculo Militar", and brother of the TV presenter Mariana Leguizamón.\n\n
