"Image caption Bailiffs removed protesters from Grangemouth plant in October 2013\n\nFive people have been charged in connection with an anti-fracking protest at an oil refinery in Grangemouth.\n\nThe protesters climbed on to the chimney at Ineos petrochemicals plant on 9 October.\n\nPolice Scotland said two men, aged 21 and 24, had been charged in connection with the protest and would appear at Falkirk Sheriff Court on Monday.\n\nThree women, aged 40, 44 and 48, were charged with breach of the peace and will also appear at court.\n\nThe 40-year-old was also charged with breach of the peace.\n\nThe other three were arrested in connection with a protest outside a hotel in Glasgow on 10 October.
