"Eric Brewe\n\nEric Brewe is an American novelist, short story writer, and screenwriter. He wrote the screenplay for the feature film "Circle of Friends" and adapted the novel "Norwegian Wood" for film as well. His novel "Vodka Is My Boyfriend" was a finalist for the PEN Center USA award for fiction in 2005.\n\nHe grew up in Los Angeles, and is the son of former New York Knick point guard Bill Bradley and actress Kathryn Grant.\n\n
