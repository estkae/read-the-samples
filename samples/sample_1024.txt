Dennis Busch\n\nDennis Busch (born 9 August 1989) is a German footballer who plays for VfB Germania Halberstadt.\n\nHe made his professional debut for Kickers Offenbach in the 3. Liga on 26 July 2010, coming on as a substitute in the 87th minute for Stefano Celozzi in the 1–1 away draw against 1. FC Saarbrücken.\n\n
