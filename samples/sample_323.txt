Jane's All the World's Aircraft\n\nJane's All the World's Aircraft is an annual publication on the subject of civil and military aircraft.\n\nIt was originally produced by Charles and John Whitley in 1909. Since 1962, it has been published by Jane's Information Group. It is published in English and French editions.\n\nAt the time of writing (June 2018), the 2018 edition is in its 75th year of publication.\n\n
